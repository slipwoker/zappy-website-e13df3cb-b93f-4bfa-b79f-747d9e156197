A gentle setup, in four steps.

1

Define the learning purpose

Start by clearly specifying appropriate use cases for your math learning environment. Identify which topics, grade levels, and learning objectives align with your educational goals. This ensures the platform serves your students' needs while maintaining focus on meaningful mathematical exploration.

2

Choose routing boundaries

Establish clear scoping and least-privilege access controls. Define which resources are available, set appropriate permissions, and ensure students interact only with vetted, age-appropriate content. This creates a safe, controlled environment for mathematical discovery.

3

Connect your environment

Follow our simple integration guidance to link MathScholars with your existing systems. We provide clear, step-by-step instructions that respect your technical setup. No complex coding required—just straightforward configuration that gets you up and running quickly.

4

Review and maintain

Conduct periodic checks to ensure everything runs smoothly. Monitor usage patterns, review access logs, and maintain responsible operations. Regular reviews help you optimize the learning experience and keep your environment secure and effective.

Our dedicated support team is available to guide you through every step—reach out anytime via our contact page.
